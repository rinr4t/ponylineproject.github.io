Welcome to the show

Introduction

This website aims to cover the entire lore of My Little Pony:Friendship is Magic and it's spinoff, My Little Pony Equestria Girls.

Sources

There's a lot of content. Look, this show ran from October 2010 to October 2019. So, what sources are considered canon in this chronology?

Any fan content is considered non-canon, and will not be included in this chronology.

Forming Equestria

The inhabitants of this land are split into Earth ponies, unicorn, and pegasi. The Earth ponies farmed, and the Pegasi controlled the weather. In return for controlling the weather, the Pegasi got food. Similarly, the unicorns got food for raising the sun and moon with their magic. None of the tribes really care about each other, as long as everyone is fed. The respective rulers of each race are:

  • Earth ponies: Chancellor Puddinghead
    • Smart Cookie
  • Unicorns: Princess Platinum
    • Clover the Clever
  • Pegasi: Commander Hurricane
    • Private Pansy

A blizzard comes one day, and creates a food shortage. This blizzard is created by three Windigos, which are ghostly horses that feed on quarrelling and disharmony, and create blizzards where they go. The tribe leaders go on a jounrey to find a new land for their people, but they all discover what will be Equestria at the same time. Their quarreling attracts the attention of the Windigos, bringing the blizzard to their newfound land. Clover the Clever recalls that Star Swirl the Bearded taught her about how Windigos work. The tribe leaders were going to freeze to death, but their wingmen Smart Cookie, Clover the Clever, and Private Pansy work together to melt the ice surrounding their leaders, creating the Fire of Friendship. Now thawed out of the ice, the three tribes agree to live in harmony, calling this newfoundland Equestria

Ancient equestria

Celstia and Luna

Two sisters, Celestia and Luna, are crowned princesses. They go on to move into the newly built Castle of Two Sisters, and keep a journal. They meet the academic and moon/sun riser Star Swirl the Bearded, who teaches them how to do his job. Star Swirl will go on to leave them in charge of the sun and moon, giving them their cutie marks. Star Swirl will go on to teach them magic as well. Starswirl is easily the smartest magician of his era - creating over 200 spells, and having a wing in the canterlot archives dedicated to him.

Princess Amore, a very distant relative of Princess Cadence, rules over the Crystal Empire and creates the first Crystal Faire, an annual event (elaborate here please)

Canterlot is built, and the day the construction completes is celebrated with the Grand Galloping Gala each year. Within Equestria there is the pegasus city Timbuktu, ruled by King Orion.

Tirek and Scorpan

The centaur Tirek and his friend, the gargoyle prince Scorpan, go on a journey to steal the magic of the ponies in Equestria. Scorpan changes his mind however, appreciating and respecting the pony way. He betrayes Tirek, turning him in. The princesses banish him to Tartarus (what is Tartarus?)

Three sirens invade Equestria. Through their singing powers, they cause disharmony and arguing between ponies. The sirens feed on this negative energy. Star Swirl the Bearded and two of his friends, Mage Meadowbrook and Somnambula work together to stop them, and Star Swirl banishes the sirens to another world without magic. Without magic, they can't cause any harm. Right?

Discord, a draconequus, overthrows Celestia and Luna with his lover Cosmos. he plants plunderseeds to drain the magic of the Tree of Harmony. Celestia and Luna used the Elements of Harmony from the tree to turn Discord to stone.

Sombra

The unicorn Sombra overthrows Princess Amore, becoming a tyrannical dictator over the Crystal Empire. Celestia and Luna defeat Sombra, and banish him beneath the ice of the Arctic North.

Luna's transformation

Luna becomes jealous of Celestia, as Equestria's ponies embrace in Celestia's day but sleep in Luna's night. She wants it to be night forever, and transofrms into Nightmare Moon, hellbent on making the night eternal.Celestia has no choice but to use the Elements of Harmony to banish her to the moon (elaborate more on here)

A young Granny Smith discovers zap apples, and founds Ponyville. The Apple family will start to have a feud with the Pear family.

Queen Chrysalis with her army of Changelings take over Timbucktu, siezing King Orion's crown. Celestia defeats Chrysalis and banishes her inside a volcano.

    Equestria Girls: Rainbow Rocks

    Back in the land of Canterlot High after our first movie, three girls from Equestria witness the magical explosion from the first movie. They realise that there's Equestrian magic in this world, and make their way towards it.

    6 moons after the explosion, Canterlot High is having a musical showcase. Sunset Shimmer is trying to redeem herself after being the school's queen bee for years, but the student body is still pretty reluctant to include her into things. Twilight's friends - the human Pinkie Pie, Rainbow Dash, Applejack, Rarity, and Fluttershy let Sunset into their circle though. Sunset spectates as her friends perform their new song. They've started a band called the Rainbooms. When playing their song, they start to have what I can really only describe as magical girl transformations but... ponified? Those who are Pegasus in Equestria grow wings, and they all have magically longer hair and sprout pony ears.

    Sunset Shimmer volunteers to show some new students around - she figures she should start in their good books as her reputation proceeds her. Turns out our new students are the three girls from before: Adagio Dazzle, Sonata Dusk, and Aria Blaze. Sunset mentions the musical showcase to them, which piques their interest.

    Sunset raises concerns about the girls to her friends, saying something is "off". And then, Adagio, Aria, and Sonata burst into the cafeteria into a song. Turns out these girls can like, hypnotise people with their songs through magical amulet necklaces - Sunset actually asks about the necklaces when showing them around but Adagio gets all defensive about it. This song entrances all the students into priortising the upcoming Musical Showcase, which they distort to become a Battle of the Bands. As they sing, the competitive tension grows and the students start to argue, forming green mist around themselves. The singing trio enjoy this, feeding off this negative energy - the green mist is sucked into their amulets. Sunset and her friends realise something is definitely up with these girls, being the only ones immune to this spellsong. They realise later that their immunity is related to the magic that caused them to gain pony features earlier.

    It turns out everyone is under this spell - when Sunset and her friends try to raise concerns about the Dazzlings to Luna and Celestia, they proclaim the Dazzlings are innocent whilst having glowing green eyes. It seems that the Rainbooms and Sunset will have to deal with the girls herself.

    Whilst Sunset may have been a student of Celestia's, she knows that she'll need some help. However, the portal to Equestria has been closed off. The portal isn't the only way to communicate to Equestria though...

    Sunset Shimmer finds a journal that she used to write messages to Celestia to in her locker. This journal would appear as a book in Celestia's library. Maybe she could reach Twilight through here? She gives it a shot, and starts writing.

    Season 5

    Uh oh, we have a communist on our hands

    In Twilight's new Castle of Friendship, the meeting room has a map of Equestria. The map shows where friendship problems are occuring - when Twilight and her friends are needed on a mission, their cutie marks glow. All of the Mane 6's cutie marks glow and floating images of them point towards somewhere on the map. The Mane 6 follow the map, and this leads them to Our Town: where all the ponies have the same cutie marks of an equal sign.

    The village's founder is known as Starlight Glimmer, who advertises her town as unique and a display of "true friendship". Our Town is based off equality - um, like, stripped of individuality levels of equality. Starlight belives that when no one is special, everything is equal, and there is harmony. Starlight and the villagers invite the Mane 6 to join the town, giving up their cutie marks in the process. They refuse, but as there is a problem in Our Town they decide to stay at the town for a bit more to see whether they can figure out how to resolve the map's calling.

    They have lunch, bickering over the controversial premise of Our Town. The (not very good, considering this town lacks talent) baker Sugar Belle overhears, and asks if their friendship is ending over this slight disagreement. They explain that conflict in natural, and Sugar Belle is intriguied by the new ideas that the Mane 6 have brought. In Sugar Belle's basement, the Mane 6 meet with Sugar Belle and her fellow townmates Party Favor and Night Glider. They admire the Mane 6's cutie marks, yeanring about their own. Eventually, they open up, revealing that Starlight uses a "Staff of Sameness" to cutie unmark ponies, apparently, it's a beautiful experience. They reveal that they're not barred from the cutie marks, and they can visit them when they wish to remind themselves of how good unmarked life is. Twilight sees this cutie unmarking as Starlight suppressing the ponies identities - and the Mane 6 make a visit to the cave.

    Starlight is happy to show them the cutie mark vault. Knowing that the Mane 6 could compromise Our Town, she takes their cutie marks, locking them away in the vault.

    The Mane 6 are locked in a village house, and have lost their talents. Twilight suggests that pretending to accept themselves as fully indoctrinated in Our Town is the best solution, and they nominate Fluttershy to be the mole - everyone else had been acting suspiciously towards the village earlier.

    Fluttershy is welcomed by Starlight, but only if she gives up the names of the ponies who told the Mane 6 about the cutie mark vault. Before Fluttershy can protest though, Party Favour takes the blame, insisting he was acting alone. Starlight locks him with the Mane 6 who are slowly giving in: it's a good thing Fluttershy is still out there.

    Fluttershy sneaks out of Starlight's house to retrieve the Mane 6's cutie marks. She notices Starlight, and spies on her. Starlight trips over a bucket of water, which soaks her "cutie mark", revealing that her equality mark is faked with makeup.

    Fluttershy splashes Starlight with water the next morning, but it doesn't get her cutie mark wet enough to show the real star swirl cutie mark. It does cause some of the makeup to run however - Party Favor comes to the rescue, and uses a rag to wipe off the rest of the makeup. Party Favor was released earlier in the morning after showing "remorse". The villagers are upset, and go to the vault to retrieve their cutie marks. Why should they live with those equal cutie marks when their own leader doesn't have one? Double Diamond shatters the vault's glass with a wooden stick the Staff of Sameness, and the villagers get their cutie marks back.

    The Mane 6's cutie marks still lie with Starlight - at this point, Starlight has escaped through a secret passage under her bed with the cutie marks. It doesn't take long for the villagers and the Mane 6 to break into her house, find the passage, and follow Starlight in a chase throughout the mountains. She gets owned by skis and snow, and drops the jars - the cutie marks float back to the Mane 6. Starlight escapes into a network of tunnels in the mountain. The Mane 6's cutie marks glow shortly afterwards, signalling that their mission to spread friendship to Our Town is done.
